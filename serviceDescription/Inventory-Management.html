Inventory Management

This service will manage your company's inventory by keeping track of how many products you have left in stock after a customer purchases that certain product. You can set a limit of each product and if the product goes under the limit, you will get a warning. Additionally, the service ensures efficient inventory management by updating stock levels after each customer purchase, allowing you to maintain optimal product quantities. You have the flexibility to set limits for each product, triggering warnings when stock falls below the designated threshold, ensuring timely restocking and preventing potential shortages.
